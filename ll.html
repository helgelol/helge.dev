It can sometimes feel like the only way to succeed is to do what's safe

and rely on decisions that we know will benefit us even if they makes us unhappy.

No matter what the benefit is, if you aren't atleast trying to do what makes you happy,

then it's only a matter of time before it starts to wear you down.

We only get to live one life,
and time is precious.

So if you can only choose one thing to do with it,

choose something that makes you happy.
